    How to play

    1. Launch the ball up the right lane and back into the table from the top.
    2. Use left and right flippers to keep the ball alive and send it back upfield.
    3. Hit bumpers and upper lanes to build score and earn extra balls at 1500 and 3500 points.
    4. Drain all balls and submit your Springfield hero name for the leaderboard.

    Audio unlocks on your first tap, click, or key press.
